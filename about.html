Meet Crystal & Manisha

Artisan Roaster began as a conversation between two friends over a mediocre cup of coffee. Crystal, a seasoned roaster with an eye for the perfect bean, and Manisha, an interior designer with a passion for community spaces, realized they shared a singular vision.

They didn't just want to open another café; they wanted to create a sanctuary. A place where the "Artisan" isn't just in the name, but in every hand-poured ceramic mug and every meticulously timed roast.
